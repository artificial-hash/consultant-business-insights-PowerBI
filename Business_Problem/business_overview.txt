PROJECT TITLE:
Consultant-Level End-to-End Business Intelligence Solution

BUSINESS CONTEXT:
A retail company operates across multiple regions and sells a variety of products.
Management wants to understand sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior
to make data-driven decisions.

BUSINESS OBJECTIVES:
1. Track overall sales and profit performance.
2. Identify underperforming regions and products.
3. Analyze customer contribution to revenue.
4. Discover trends over time for better forecasting.
5. Provide actionable insights to management.

KEY STAKEHOLDERS:
- Business Managers
- Sales Team
- Finance Team
- Strategy Team

SUCCESS METRICS:
- Increase profitability
- Reduce losses from low-performing products
- Improve regional performance
- Better decision-making using dashboards
